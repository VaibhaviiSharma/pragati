About Jaipur

Jaipur, also known as the Pink City, is known for its rich culture,and royal architecture. It was founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II. .

As you roam around the busy and chaotic streets of this beautiful city, you'll be attracted by the beauty of grand palaces, ancient forts, and markets that offer a great shopping experience. Jaipur is a city that has both tranditional handicrafts and modern things.And if you are in jaipur market, you cannot resist yourself from buying goods from the market.

Top Places to Visit in Jaipur

Hawa Mahal

'Hawa mahal' or the palace of winds is one of the most beautiful architectures of jaipur. It was built in 1799 by Maharaj sawai pratap singh.The unique architecture of this palace allow it to remain cold even in the hot summer days. This palace was actualy designed for the womens of the royal household to see from this palace .

City Palace

this is located in the heart of Jaipur, the City Palace is a great mixture of Mughal and Rajput architecture. The palace complex houses museums, courtyards, and gardens, it gives visitors a glimpse in the royal lifestyle.

Amber Fort

located on a hilltop, Amber Fort is one of the most iconic destination of jaipur. The fort's mesmerising structure, beautiful mirror works, and the iconic Maota lake make it a must-visit for someone who is exploring the city.
